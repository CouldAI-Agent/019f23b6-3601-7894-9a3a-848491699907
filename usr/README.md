# Fool of Star

Fool of Star is a high-performance, ultra-secure, end-to-end encrypted global multi-currency payment application, personal wallet, and private trading ecosystem. It features an integrated, privacy-first personal AI assistant and a native private app coin for global trade.

## Features
- **Absolute Privacy & Zero-Trust Security**: End-to-end encryption for all transactions and messages.
- **The Personal Wallet System**: Multi-currency wallet for fiat (USD, EUR, INR) and stablecoins, with an easy-to-use balance view.
- **Personal AI Assistant**: Chat with your local, privacy-first AI to perform transactions and get financial coaching.
- **Private App Coin & Global Trading**: Swap fiat or digital assets into the native app coin instantly with minimal slippage.
- **Zero-Knowledge Brokerage**: 1% encrypted brokerage fee managed seamlessly in the background.

## Tech Stack
- Flutter (iOS, Android, Web, Desktop)

## Setup
1. Ensure you have Flutter installed.
2. Run `flutter pub get`.
3. Run `flutter run` to launch the app on your preferred platform.
